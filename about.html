About Snake Pro

Snake Pro is a modern HTML5 snake game designed for both mobile devices and desktop browsers. The game offers smooth controls, responsive gameplay, and an enjoyable experience for players of all ages.

Features

  • 🐍 Smooth snake movement
  • 📱 Mobile-friendly controls
  • 🎁 Bonus food system
  • 🏆 High score leaderboard
  • 🎨 Multiple skins
  • ⚡ Fast and lightweight gameplay

How to Play

Control the snake, eat food to grow longer, avoid hitting yourself, and achieve the highest score possible.

Developer

Snake Pro is independently developed and continuously improved to provide a better gaming experience.
